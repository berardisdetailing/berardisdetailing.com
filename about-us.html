About Us


Michael started out as a detailer at a Auto Body Facility in the early 90's. Michael was trained by World Class skilled technicians in the Auto Body and detailing field.

While working at this Body shop Michael had a goal, a dream, and a wish that one day he would open up a detail shop-auto body shop. This dream didn't come easy for Michael-it took a lot of hard work and dedication and a big savings to open up Berardi's.

Michael was so dedicated that he bought his first truck and started going to business's and houses and detailing on-site.

Who else would detail cars from 7am until the following day to 6am?  Michael did what he had to do to fulfill his dream of success.

He would go to an Italian restaurant on the Main Line in Wayne and wash cars in the back of a parking lot, earning very little to make a savings for the business.  He would work from 5pm to 1 am approaching people that were heading in to have dinner, offering them a car wash. Michael's dedication and old world work ethic, that he learned from his family, was truly inspiring.

From there Michael began detailing cars in his driveway at his house. He opened his first shop at the Westtown Gulf in West Chester PA, where he has been operating for over 10 years.

Michael went on to open his second shop, which is now the Berardi's headquarters, located next to Jimmy John's Hotdogs.

Today Michael is proud to have a promising business and highly skilled staff, famous for making cars look showroom new. It is a one stop shop facility that specializes in expert auto body, meticulous detailing, bumper repair, paintless dent repair, auto glass replacement, interior repair, window tinting, wheel repair, pinstriping, and custom wheels and accessories.

Berardi's has received the highest praise from leading auto manufacturers, for quality and customer satisfaction. Berardi's prides themselves on expert quality, sincerity, integrity, and the tenacity and dedication that began Michael's journey. 

When you walk into our doors your not just a customer, you are truly part of the Berardi family and that's what sets us apart from the others.
