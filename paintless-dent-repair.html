Paintless Dent Repair

Looking for the best way to repair those dings and dents?
For many reasons, paintless dent removal (PDR) is the best choice to remove dings and dents from your vehicle. Not only will you save time and money, but your vehicles original paint finish will be preserved while eliminating the need for expensive materials involved with painting.

PRR is a fraction of the cost typically (one-third) of a traditional auto body repair or replacement. No painting or filler is needed and the majority of the jobs are done in less than 2 hours. Our highly skilled technicians have many years of experience, and customers are often amazed at their ability to restore a vehicles value and appearance in such short time.

What exactly is Paintless Dent Removal?
PDR is a technique used to remove dings and dents from vehicles using special tools, rods, and reflective sources. PDR is very effective when your vehicles paint has not been cracked or damaged. A dent is meticulously massaged from the inside of the damaged panel, eventually leading to a undetectable repair. This is all done without the use of body fillers (such as bondo) or painting, which has the added benefit of protecting your vehicles original finish.

If your car has sustained a large or deep dent, or if the damage is across a body line, our skilled technicians can restore your panel to very near it's original shape, undetectable to all but the closest inspection. Fortunately, if we are unable to remove damage to your satisfaction using PDR, we will credit the price toward a traditional auto body repair involving repainting. Therefore giving PDR a chance to remove damage is a no-risk option!
