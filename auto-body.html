Expert Auto Body & Painting


Berardi's Auto Body is the Tri-State Areas Premier Auto Body Repair Facility. Serving clients from Pennsylvania, New Jersey, Delaware, and Maryland. Berardi's is your one stop headquarters when you get into a major collision, a minor fender bender, or get a small dent, or need your vehicle repainted.

Our tools include the newest, most sophisticated and innovative equipment available. With years in the auto body industry our highly skilled staff will handle all the details to make sure that your automobile is repaired to factory standards(Pre accident condition). We also take care of all insurance claims, writing the estimate, and arranging a rental car.

Berardi's also does out of pocket repairs for those who wish not to go through there insurance companies. Berardi's also provides free towing and free pick up and delivery from your home or office. Berardi's deals with all major insurance companies. All auto body and paintwork that we perform comes with a written lifetime warranty. Berardi's auto body repairs all makes and models.
